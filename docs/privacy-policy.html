Overview

Carvixa is an offline vehicle maintenance and fuel tracking application. We respect your privacy and keep app data under your control.

Information Entered by the User

You may enter vehicle details, mileage, maintenance records, fuel entries, dates, costs, and optional notes. This information is used only to provide app features on your device.

Local Storage

Vehicle details, maintenance history, fuel records, theme preferences, and settings are stored locally on your device and are not sent to our servers.

Personal Data

Carvixa does not require registration and does not ask for your name, phone number, home address, payment information, contacts, or precise location.

Device Permissions

The app does not require access to your camera, microphone, contacts, messages, call history, precise location, or personal media files for its core functionality.

Internet, Analytics, and Advertising

Carvixa does not require a backend server, advertising network, analytics platform, or third-party tracking service for its core features.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your locally stored vehicle, maintenance, or fuel information with third parties.

Data Deletion

You can delete available records inside the app. Uninstalling the app may also remove locally stored data, subject to your device backup settings.
