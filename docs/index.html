About Carvixa

Carvixa helps vehicle owners track mileage, maintenance schedules, fuel entries, service history, and general vehicle care information. The app works locally on the user's device.

Main Features

  • Vehicle dashboard and mileage tracking.
  • Maintenance reminders and service status.
  • Fuel fill-up history and estimated consumption.
  • Maintenance and fuel history records.
  • Local settings and dark mode.
